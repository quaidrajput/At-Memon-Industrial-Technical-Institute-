Why This Camp?

Hearing loss silently destroys futures. Students miss what teachers say. Teachers miss what students need. Families suffer without knowing help exists. WHO estimates 15 million Pakistanis have hearing loss — most are undiagnosed.

  • ⚙ Free Hearing Aid Check-up
